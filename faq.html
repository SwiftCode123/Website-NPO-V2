FAQs

How do I get started?

You can get started by simply filling out a workshop registration form for any type of workshop you want. Furthermore, if you have any questions, you can email, call or just leave a comment in the contacts page.

Why Girl You Matter?

By partnering with this organization, we can help build you that needed confidence during the transition from middle school to high school. Therefore, we can help you in ways such as giving you tips, talking about anything or even just having a small conversation.

Why you matter?

Every race and girl matters and that is what we care about at this organization. We want to help as many people as possible in the big transition from middle school to high school.

Any cost to registration?

As this is a non-profit organization, we do not take any kind of fees. Everything is free and we do not make any profit. Just helping people.
